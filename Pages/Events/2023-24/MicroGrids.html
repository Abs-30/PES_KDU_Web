Microgrids

"Concept, Purpose And Applications"

On October 17th, 2023, we had the privilege of hosting a captivating guest lecture on Microgrids - "Concept, Purpose, and Applications." The event took place at the prestigious FOE 6-2, showcasing the collaborative efforts of the IEEE PES chapter and the Department of Electrical, Electronic, and Telecommunication.

Our distinguished lecturer, Prof.(Ms) Lidula N. Widanagama Arachchige, is a renowned authority in the field of electrical engineering. Her expertise in Microgrids provided attendees with a comprehensive understanding of this innovative technology that is reshaping the energy landscape.
