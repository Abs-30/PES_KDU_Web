Genesiz '23 by ERIC KDU

We are excited to take you back to the dynamic and enriching experience of Genesiz 2023, a remarkable inter-university competition held at the KDU Rathmalana premises on October 8, 2023. This outstanding event was jointly hosted by ERIC KDU, in collaboration with IEEE PES, ComSoc, EMBS, Rotaract Club of KDU, and BCS KDU, bringing together a diverse range of talents and interests.

Genesiz 2023 was more than just a competition; it was a platform for intellectual exchange and collaboration. This event celebrated the fusion of knowledge, innovation, and the spirit of camaraderie among participants from various universities. In the spirit of collaboration, IEEE PES KDU actively participated in the Brainforge quiz, a collaborative endeavor that challenged the brightest minds in power and energy engineering. The competition provided a unique space for these aspiring engineers to test their knowledge, exchange ideas, and strengthen their bonds with like-minded peers.
