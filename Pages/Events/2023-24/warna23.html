Magnet Wax painting stall at වර්ණ '23

FOE Art Circle proudly presented 'වර්ණ'23' a debut art exhibition that took place on the 2nd and 3rd of August 2023. This vibrant event transformed the creative landscape with an eclectic array of art and crafts. Among the diverse exhibits, the Power and Energy Society carved a niche with their innovative stall showcasing captivating magnet wax paintings. Enthusiastic students flocked to the stall, eagerly immersing themselves in the world of magnetic artistry. The fusion of colors and the interplay of energies came alive as participants gained hands-on experience, leaving an indelible mark on their creative spirits.
