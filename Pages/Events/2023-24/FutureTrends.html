Future Trends In Engineering

On the august evening of August 11th, 2023, within the splendid confines of the FDSS auditorium, the collaborative efforts of the IEEE PES chapter and the Department of Electrical Electronic and Telecommunication gave rise to a remarkable guest lecture. Prof. Chandima Gomes took the stage as a harbinger of future engineering trends, his words painting a vivid picture of upcoming technological frontiers — from artificial intelligence and sustainable energy to quantum marvels. The ensuing dialogue during the Q&A session amplified the evening's impact, invigorating minds and spirits alike. This convergence of visionary insights and inquisitive minds crystallized into an event that would undoubtedly shape the trajectory of innovation for years to come.
