Guest Lecture on Wireless Power Transmission

The online event organized by IEEE PES KDU on “Say Goodbye to the Mess of Cables: exploring Wireless Power Transmission” was a highly engaging and enlightening session. The event took place on 24th October 2022, and it drew a diverse audience interested in the latest advancements in power transmission technology. Eng. Devaka Jayawardana, a distinguished expert in this field, served as the keynote speaker for the event. He highlighted the major challenges associated with traditional wired power transmission and how wireless solutions could revolutionize. Overall, this online event on wireless power transmission was a grand success, offering attendees a glimpse into the future of power technology.
