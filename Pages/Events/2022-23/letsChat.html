Lets' Chat

The “Let’s Chat” session organized by IEEE PES KDU was a crucial and timely gathering aimed at addressing the pressing issue of the current power crisis in the country. Held on 19th of July 2022, the session brought different personalities to a friendly discussion about the prevailing power crisis within the country. All were set in finding solutions such as solar PV and electricity generation using wind and also mental health impacts due to this power crisis was discussed.
