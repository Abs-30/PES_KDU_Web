Electrical and Electronics Engineering Awareness Session for intake 40

The IEEE Power & Energy Society Student Branch Chapter of KDU organized an event for the new intake (Intake 40), aiming to rise awareness about the vast potential of the Electrical and Electronic engineering field. This event was held on 18th of April 2023 at the KDU premises, and we were thrilled to had Dr. HM Wijekoon Banda, a distinguished expert in the industry with the years of experience in the field. Attendees were able to gain valuable knowledge that will shape their academic and professional journey and had a fantastic opportunity to explore the limitless possibilities in this field.
