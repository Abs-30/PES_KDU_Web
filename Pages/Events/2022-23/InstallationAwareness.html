Installation Ceremony and Awareness Session

The installation ceremony and awareness session of the IEEE PES in KDU were held with great enthusiasm and success. This event took place on 1st of September 2022 at FGS auditorium, and it marked a significant milestone for the society as the new executive committee and board of directors were officially installed into their roles. During the awareness session, the goals and objectives of IEEE PES KDU were presented, and it emphasized the society’s commitment to promoting knowledge, research, and innovation in the power and energy domain. The keynote speaker, Dr. W.D.A.S. Rodrigo delivered an insightful speech on the latest trends, challenges, and opportunities in the field, inspiring the audience to pursue careers in this dynamic industry.
